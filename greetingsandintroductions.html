🗣️ Lesson 1: Greetings and Introductions

Learn how to greet and introduce yourself in Shona. Below you'll find key phrases with audio and pronunciation tips.

📚 Key Vocabulary

Hello
Mhoro / Maswera sei
Pronounced: mho-ro / ma-swe-ra say
Good morning
Mangwanani
Pronounced: man-gwa-na-ni

💬 Sample Dialogue

A: Mhoro! Wakadini zvako?

B: Ndiri right, wakadini zvako?

A: Ndiri bhoo, ndinofara kukuziva.

B: Ndinofara kukuziva futi!

📝 Practice Tip

Listen to the pronunciation and repeat out loud. Practice with a partner if possible. Say "Mhoro" to someone today!
